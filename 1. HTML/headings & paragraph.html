Main Title - h1

This is the most important heading, usually used once per page (e.g., page title).

Section Title - h2

Use h2 for main sections under h1.

Subsection Title - h3

Use h3 for subsections under h2.

Sub-subsection Title - h4

Use h4 for details under h3 sections.

Note or Minor Section - h5

h5 is used for minor headings or detailed categories.

Smallest Heading - h6

h6 is the least important heading and smallest in size.
